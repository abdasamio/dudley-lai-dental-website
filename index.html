Restorative Dentistry

Restorative dentistry repairs and replaces damaged or missing teeth for a healthy smile.

  • Fillings – Decay
  • Fillings – Chipped Tooth
  • Dental Crowns
  • Dental Bridges
  • Dental Implants
  • Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Periodontal Treatment

Periodontal treatment cares for your gums and helps prevent or treat gum disease.

  • Scaling and Root Planing
  • Gum Disease Diagnosis
  • Maintenance Cleanings
  • Patient Education
  • Antibacterial Therapy
  • Gum Health Monitoring
Learn More

Orthodontics

Orthodontics straightens teeth and corrects jaw alignment for a healthier smile.

  • Traditional Braces
  • Ceramic Braces
  • Clear Aligners
  • Early Intervention for Children
  • Retainers
  • Bite Correction
Learn More

Preventative Dental Care

Preventative dental care protects your teeth and gums through regular check-ups and cleaning.

  • Regular Check-Ups
  • Professional Cleaning

  • Dental X-Rays
  • Oral Health Education
  • Fissure Sealants
